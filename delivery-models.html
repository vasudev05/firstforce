Delivery Models

Delivery Models :

FirstForce offer the following models to fulfil several projects for our customers. The client has the best high-quality to choose from any of these models and to customize as per his needs. RDC (Remote Development Centre) A RDC is an engagement model where we recreate an offshore advance environment at our facility in Irvine (US) which meets your exact business requirements, both in technology and manpower. It is an essential allowance of your own organization. We employee all suitable team members allowing to your requirements and they would operate within our IT infrastructure. We guarantee a rapid start-up of your operations in the most cost-effective method. An RDC is fit for organizations that need to essence on their core-competencies and however retain full control of all their IT creativities and secure all IP rights.

Advantages:
  • It Deals 40-50% cost savings related to traditional development at onsite
  • Fixed cost every month
  • Better control over development process
  • Better Control on offshore development team
  • Low risk with high return
  • Less organization care required for management of IT projects
Onsite Development Centre:

A dedicated team of professional’s works at the client’s facility, and reports to the client’s IT head. The real project managing could also be done by the client, if wanted. This is suitable for organizations, which already have IT facilities in place, and is useful for projects like on-going software upgrades that require constant interaction with different departments at the client’s location. This delivers the client from personnel staffing & management, while confirming development by quality and technology savvy professionals. We are fitted out to meet desires of various sizes and at various locations.

Offsite Development Centre

Under this structure, the development of projects could be undertaken at our local development center nearest to the client location. This allows the client to manage the project management.
